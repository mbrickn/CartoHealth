Hospital Distribution

Hosptials are important healthcare infrastructure. This map highlights major hospitals and clinics in Ohio and surrounding areas.

However what this map truly shows is where hospitals are not. Poor neighborhoods and rural communities are often underserved, as can be seen here.

Note: For data clarity, small features have been hidden at far zooms.
